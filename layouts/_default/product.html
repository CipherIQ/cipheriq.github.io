Correlation Engine

Automatically matches static CBOM assets against runtime observations. Detects shadow cryptography, configuration drift, and algorithms that aren't in your bill of materials.

Policy Engine

Define cryptographic policies. Continuous evaluation against live data. Automated alerting when endpoints violate compliance requirements. Built-in CNSA 2.0 policy templates.

Per-endpoint and organization-wide scoring of post-quantum readiness. Track migration progress. Identify the highest-risk assets. Generate compliance reports.

Quantum-Safe Transport

Agent-to-collector communication uses gRPC over HTTP/2 with TLS 1.3, mutual TLS authentication, and pre-shared key support for quantum-safe key establishment. We practice what we preach — CipherIQ's own transport is designed for the post-quantum era.
